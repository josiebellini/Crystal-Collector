Crystal Collector

STEP 1

You will be given a random number at ther start of the game.

STEP 2

By clicking on one of the 4 crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

STEP 3

You win the game by macthing your total score to the random number, but be careful... don't go above!

NOTES

The value of eaach crystal is hidden until you click on it, and the values change when the game resets.
